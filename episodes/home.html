About the Podcast

Stuff You Should Know, often abbreviated as SYSK, is a free, award-winning podcast and video series published by the HowStuffWorks website and hosted by Josh Clark and Charles W. "Chuck" Bryant, both writers at H o wStuttWorks.

The podcast, released every Tuesday and Thursday, educates listeners on a wide variety of topics, often using popular culture as a reference giving the podcast comedic value

Facts about the show:

  • Show is downloaded more than I million times per week.
  • Hasbro released a special edition of Trivial Pursuit: The Stuff You Should Know Edition based on episodes from the podcast.
  • Josh and Chuck receive over 350 pieces of fan main a week.
